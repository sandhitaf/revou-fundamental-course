International Travel

Japan

Japan is well-known for easy-access, beautiful nature, and delicious cuisine. There are also many historical and modern architecture.

South Korea

South Korea is warm and welcoming with rich heritage and culture. It offers many other cultural and artistic attractions, such as Museum of Art and other.

Singapore

Singapore is a country with a wonderful tropical climate, vibrant nightlife, fantastic shopping, and fascinating historical trivia.

Domestic Travel

Bali

Bali, the famed Island of the Gods, with its varied landscape of hills and mountains, rugged coastlines, sandy beaches and lush rice terraces.

Bandung

Bandung is a large city set amid volcanoes and tea plantations. It is known for colonial and art deco architecture with a lot of overwhelming destination.

Jakarta

Jakarta is a huge city with several district containing sightseeing, restaurant, nightlife and the most popular landmark: the Monas Obelisk which has a long story.
